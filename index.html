The Rukmani Birla Modern High School was established at Jaipur in 2003. The co-educational senior-secondary school has over 2,300 students and is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The school is ISO 9001:2000 certified for quality management.
Spread across 20 acres, the school houses a sports complex with facilities such as an athletics track, football and hockey fields, basketball courts as well as courts for games such as volley ball, lawn tennis and badminton. An air-conditioned gymnasium and multi-purpose auditorium wi th a seating capacity of 1,000 are some of the school’s noteworthy facilities.

Covid-19 Precautions At The School

Safe School Strategy (contd..)
• COVID appropriate behavior such as: Required do’s and don’ts including maintenance of hand hygiene, respiratory hygiene and disinfection of frequently touched surfaces, Maintaining physical distance, Hygienic practices necessary for the prevention of COVID, Thermal screening for fever detection, Avoiding attending school if having symptoms and seeking medical care were encouraged.
• We maintained flexible, staggered, and reduced timings for different classes
• Only 50% of the students allowed to attend the school
• The School did not undertake school events where physical/social distancing is not possible.
• Functions and celebration of festivals were all Online. 9
• Virtual parent teacher meetings & counselling sessions for students organized online.
• NEW Admission interaction were done with staggered timings or on virtual mode.
• Parent’s/Guardian’s consent was made mandatory before any student was allowed school.
• Follow-up on the progress of the learning outcomes of all such students planned appropriately.
• Sensitized students, parents & all school staff on COVID-19 related challenges and their role
• Staff & students belonging to containment zones advised not to attend the school until the containment zone is denotified.
• All employees who are at higher risk told to take extra precautions.
• Attendance not enforced and depended entirely on parental consent.
• Arogya Setu App made compulsory
• Ensured continuous maintenance & monitoring of cleanliness and hygienic conditions in & around school premises
• Simple health screening of students and staff carried out daily, and updates maintained by the school nurse.
• Ensured availability of necessary equipment like gloves, face cover/ masks, hand washing soaps for sanitation
• Board Practical work done in small groups maintaining physical distance.

About

Rukmani Birla Modern High School Jaipur, is a Senior Secondary School (XI-XII), affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The School is a Coed Day School, with classes from Nursery to XII. It is an English Medium school. The school is located in Durgapura area of Jaipur. Rukmani Birla Modern High School was established in . It is a Public School and is managed by Rukmani Birla EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTE.

Rukmani Birla Modern High School Durgapura Shanti Nagar is a Coed Day School, which services the educational need of hundreds of students from Nursery through XII. The classes in Rukmani Birla Modern High School for every academic year starts from April to March.
